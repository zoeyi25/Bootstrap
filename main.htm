About



Froissantism is a monotheistic religion based on the teachings of Froissant. For far too long, croissants have been overlooked as mere blasphemously overpriced blobs of dough. Time and time again, we have proven them wrong. With the entire animal kingdom converted into croissants, humans remain as our last target. His Froissantiness leads our army on our crusade to rescue the humans from the bottomless abyss they have descended into. When the Day of Reckoning arrives, we will charge forth, armed with our rolling pins and baking tray shields, crying, “LONG LIVE CROISSANTS!” Together, we will croissantify the world.
